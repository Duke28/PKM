ABOUT US

PKM CONSULT

PKM Consult was founded in 2015 with a sole purpose of providing financial accounting and tax services. Through strength and growth, the company included compliance services. The director of the firm has extensive experience in the corporate sector, especially the financial services and has worked with various regulatory boards and major companies in the financial services industry. Understanding the rules and requirements in the game, the firm offers its services to small and medium sized companies for their growth and expansion. PKM Consult has most of its clients in the financial services industry, with few on other sectors and its strategic focus is to provide relevant and customized financial services and advice to its clients
